What is Maxcoin?

Max Speed, Security, Integrity & Governance

We are a Self Governed, tech-oriented, no hype community driven cryptocurrency. We are here for all, whether you’ve been with us since the start, or only just discovered us today. It is your coin and it’s in your hands, a true Eco-system of human interaction thru collaboration. Click here to view our Philosophy & Principles.
